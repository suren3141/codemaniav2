## INPUT

3
10 3
7 1 6
20 5
3 4 10 5 1
9 5
1 1 1 1 1

## OUTPUT

2
4
5


## EXPLANATION

There are three test cases.

- In the first case, you can chose pipe $2$ and $3$ for a total length of 7 (< 10). So, $m = 2$.

- Similarly in the second test case by choosing all the pipes except the third one, you can get $m = 4$.

- In the final test, all pipes can be taken.
